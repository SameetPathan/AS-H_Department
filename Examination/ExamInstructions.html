Instructions for Easy Check Portal
  1. Candidates shall appear for the on-line examination from the place of their convenience.
  2. For giving exam on Easy Check system, candidates need to login to https://ec.pceterp.in
  3. Candidates are recommended to use desktop/laptop device having camera and microphone.
  4. Candidate can login using their laptop, PC. Please take care that batteries of these units are properly charged prior to examination and connected to internet facility.
  5. To solve theory question paper, candidates should use one side ruled A4 size papers with their PRN no., course code, course name, page no. on every page right hand top corner.
  6. Candidates are advised to keep pages ready with duly filled correct information before the start of examination.
  7. Candidates shall use appropriate margins on all sides of pages.
  8. Candidates shall avoid writing on back to back pages.
  9. Candidates are advised to use of Blue/Black ball pen while writing answers of theory question paper.
  10. Draw neat labelled diagrams wherever necessary.
  11. Candidates are allowed to use of non programmable calculator.
  12. After writing all answers on A4 size papers, students shall take photographs of the same in sequence of page numbers and create a single pdf file of all pages together and upload it on EasyCheck platform.
  13. Candidates should verify the answer sheets PDF file before final submission.
  14. Extra time of 20 min. is provided for scanning and uploading PDF. Students are advised to start task of scanning and uploading PDF immediately after the end time for examination.
Instructions for Easy Pariksha Portal
  1. Candidates shall appear for the on-line examination from the place of their convenience.
  2. Candidates need to login into EasyPariksha system 15 minutes before examination starts.
  3. For giving exam on EasyPariksha system, candidates need to login to https://ep.pceterp.in
  4. Candidates are recommended to use desktop/laptop device having camera and microphone.
  5. Candidates can login using their laptop, PC. Please take care that batteries of these units are properly charged prior to examination and connected to internet facility.
  6. For best user experience Google Chrome browser (version: 85.0.4183.102) / Mozilla Firefox browser (version: 80.0.1) and above is recommended.
  7. Candidates are instructed to give permission to EasyPariksha system to access Candidate’s laptop/desktop device camera and microphone whenever asked by EasyPariksha system.
  8. Candidates are strictly instructed not to turn off their laptop/desktop device camera and microphone and should not tamper settings of camera and microphone till the examination is completed.
  9. To give exam on EasyPariksha software system, Candidates should have at least
  10. Username and password to use EasyPariksha system is official email id. EasyPariksha system will ask Candidates to change password on first login. It is recommended to set strong password.
  11. At the beginning of examination, Proctor/Supervisor will send video conferencing meeting link, student need to click on that link to join video conferencing meeting with Proctor/Supervisor. Candidates should not close video conferencing meeting till examination is completed.
  12. Candidates can converse with Proctor/Supervisor using the chat link, if needed to resolve issues/problems.
  13. If question has image then student can click on image itself to enlarge it.
  14. Candidates are allowed to use of non programmable calculator.
