Industrial Visit

  • First Year Engineering department is committed to ensure the overall development of our students as well as faculty.
  • In view of this,industrial visit was arranged since 2017 so that the students can get proper insight about how the real working environment of a company is and also get the exposure to the real workstations, plants.,machines and systems.
  • Industrial visit gives an opportunity to the students to Increase Practical Awareness.
  • The objective of this visit is to help the students to understand all mechanism and programming behind all the industrial processes.
  • It also help the students to gain practical experience from the industry

In view of this First year engineering department had arranged Industrial Visit to 4 different locations . Students can choose the location as per their interest.

Adlabs Imagica: “BEHIND THE RIDES”

An unique industrial visit at Imagica – Theme Park and snow park helped the students to experience the use of various engineering streams applied in practical life. Students went through a presentation by the project department of Imagica on the use of technology at Imagica. The visit gave an enlightening experience about the park right from the working of a roller coaster to the architecture of the structures. Such industrial visits help to enrich students with knowledge at the same time it also acts as a stress buster for the students having a hectic schedule.

FIAT,India

Car Plant (Car Assembly Line),Power Train Department: ,Presentation on WCM Practices .

POLYELASTICA PVT LTD. Chakan, Pune

An Industrial visit "POLYELASTICA PVT LTD”. Chakan. Pune and to “SHREE PRESSING PVT LTD”,Chakan which is around 30 km away from Pune was successfully arranged on 5 April 2019 for first year students under the subject Basic Mechanical Engineering for the academic year 2018-19.
