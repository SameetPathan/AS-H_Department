Creative Arts Sports Sessions and Seminars Workshops
Creative Arts

Creative Arts have the potential to help improve a student's memory and concentration skills, develop decision-making and critical thinking skills, enhance communication and listening skills, but also encourage focus and discipline.

Objective: To develop a sense of aesthetics and also enhance creativity, which will help them in engineering.

Students preference for the three art forms was taken in the PTG Session -I. Keeping in mind students preference and equal distribution in all art forms class teachers and PTG’s divided the students in all art form. Parallel sessions of all the 3 creative arts were taken conducted from 10 th -14 th August,each session was of 2 hrs and per day total 4 sessions were conducted. The details of the three creative arts is mentioned below:

Theater Arts

Theater Education programs provide a safe place for students to be themselves, to explore relationships, to develop compassion, collaborative practices, and the idea of delayed gratification.

Objectives
  • Personality development of students
  • Building up Stage confidence
  • Strengthening confidence by inculcating value of Education.
  • Feeling of gratitude
Outcomes
  • Students develop creativity and spontaneity by the exploration of theatre.
  • Students develop critical-thinking and problem-solving skills through acting and technical projects.
  • Students develop communication skills via acting and presentations.
  • Students explore the concepts of self, body and voice work, improvisation, acting techniques, and public performance.
Music

The resource person for music workshop was Mr Tejas Chavan.He is Music composer, arranger and recording engineer at Resonance Studio. He has last 10 years of experience in this field. He have worked with legends in music & singers like Shankar Mahadevan, Suresh Wadkar, Kailash kher and many more.He has been awarded by Kimva book of records for his composition ‘Marathi Breathless’ .

Painting
Objectives
  • To understand basic principals of design and color painting, concepts,media and formats.
  • To develop ability to demonstrate their thinking using painting.
  • To learn color scheme .
Outcomes
  • Student will be able to apply different art forms to express their thoughts .
  • Student will be able to produce creative works that demonstrate innovation in concepts.
  • Develop their drawing ability .
  • Students explore the concepts of self, body and voice work, improvisation, acting techniques, and public performance.
Sports

To Make students aware of importance of sports for leaving a healthy life. One session of sports for each division was conducted at different locations viz play ground, canteen basement, classrooms ( indoor games).

The sports activities included:
  1. Indoor games like Carom, Chess, Table Tennis, Team building Games , Introduction to Gym and various fitness equipments.
  2. Outdoor sports included were volley ball, Kho Kho,Cricket , Football , Dash ball.
  3. Team building Activity :The team building activities helped students in developing Problem solving skills.Decision-making 1.Problem solving skills.:Before reaching the ultimate decision, it's important to weigh the pros and the cons to ensure that you're making the best decision possible.
Outcomes

Students recognized the roll of sports in their life, which will helps to make their life a healthy & sporty

“Matlab and Multisim” One day Workshop

Objective of the workshop is to introduce Multisim and Matlab to AS&H Engg. Students in order to enhance their knowledge and create awareness in the field of circuit simulation.
